May I introduce... Perce.io Paparazzi.

You need screenshots of any page accessible on the internet included in your site? You want to pimp up listings of several web pages with fancy thumbnails showing the depending page? And it would be great, if you could decide in which size the images will be served? Great, then Paparazzi is something for you...

Wanna play around with it? Here we are!

  • http://paparazzi.perce.io/width/800/height/600/url/paparazzi.perce.io?scale=100

We love open source and all the great technology, that is with us every day. And that is the reason, why we decided to make this little service public. Use it! Play around with it! File issues! And if you have any troubles with it, let us know. We are glad to help.

Perce.io will be a new web application, that helps you to keep track of frontend performance metrics. Wondering how your website evolved over the last few weeks? Want to have a general overview about CSS quality and eventual bad practices? Asking your self how you JavaScript codebase behaves? Awesome, so do we!

We are working hard on it and plan to open a private beta by the middle of the year 2014. If you are interested in any progress, feel free to subscribe to our brand new newsletter. :)
